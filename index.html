ABOUT US

This is the story behind Kabuki Strength

We are a group of passionate amateur-to-elite level lifters, strength athletes, and coaches with a shared goal in bettering ourselves and growing in every facet of life. Our gym is a “laboratory” where we test cutting edge equipment and methodologies before sharing them with the rest of the world via our platform. We are welcome to anyone, as long as they share the passion of self-improvement and physical training that guides and shapes our lives, minds, and bodies. Kabuki Strength's in-house staff of professional coaches train our clients of all experience levels to be technically proficient, durable and brutally strong. We pride ourselves in being objective and finding unique solutions to unique problems for our clients. Individual factors such as training experience, movement limitations, time, personality, opportunity assessment and favourite movements are just a few of the things we consider when formulating training plans. We strive to build our training plans around scientific principles and we truly believe we fill a unique void in the market of understanding how to practically apply research to real-world situations. Backed by an advisory of world-renowned clinicians, doctors, coaches and researchers, our in-house coaching staff is ready to help you tackle your goals.
